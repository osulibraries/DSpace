Logan Elm Press Catalogue Raisonné

The OSU Libraries' Logan Elm Press fosters collaborative research opportunities, creative exploration and the construction of knowledge through the Arts of the Book - binding, printing, typography and papermaking. A Catalogue Raisonné of all the books and broadsides produced at the Press from 1978 to the present is stored in and accessible through the Knowledge Bank.
